Who Can Apply?

The People's Bot is an experiment in using robots for the public good, to broaden access to physical events.

To start, we're making it available to these three groups:

Students

High school, college, & grads: Get a People's Bot Scholarship

Media

Want to report on CHI? Apply for a Blogger/Journalism Fellowship

Anyone

We've put some time slots on Ebay, proceeds to fund student grants

Why are you doing this?

Telepresent robots are often pitched as a technology to extend the influence of those who alread have money and power.

We want to use robotic telepresence for the public good -- broadening access, supporting public interest reporting, and funding even more public interest initiatives.

Do you have further ideas? Contact us.
